  When: Don't like current reply
  Effect: Creates new reply with Ollama
  Next: Review new version

  When: Want to open in Gmail
  Effect: Shows you how to find it in Gmail
  Next: Manual review in Gmail

✕ DELETE
  When: Reply is bad/not needed
  Effect: Permanently removes draft
  Warning: Can't undo!
