# 📧 Email Assistant - User Setup Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you set up the Email Assistant and start generating AI-powered email replies.

---

## 🚀 **Quick Setup (3 Steps)**

### **Step 1: Create Your Account**

1. Go to: `https://email-assistant-xxx.onrender.com`
2. Click **"Create one here"** on login page
3. Fill in:
   - **Username**: Your preferred username
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Password**: Strong password (min 6 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll be redirected to login automatically

---

### **Step 2: Login**

1. Enter your **Username** and **Password**
2. Click **"Sign In"**
3. You'll see the **Dashboard**

---

### **Step 3: Connect Your Gmail**

1. After login, go to **Settings** → **Gmail Setup**
2. Enter your Gmail address (must be @gmail.com)
3. Click **"Connect Gmail"**
4. The agent will start monitoring your emails!

---

## 🤖 **How It Works**

```
1. You receive email
           ↓
2. Agent automatically reads it
           ↓
3. AI generates smart reply
           ↓
4. Draft saved in your Gmail
           ↓
5. You review and send!
```

## 📋 **Features**

✅ **Automatic Email Monitoring**
- Agent checks your Gmail every few minutes
- Reads unread emails

✅ **AI-Powered Replies**
- Uses Claude AI to understand context
- Generates professional, thoughtful responses

✅ **Gmail Draft Management**
- Replies saved as drafts (not sent automatically!)
- You review before sending
- Full control over what gets sent

✅ **Multi-User Support**
- Each user has their own Gmail connection
- Multiple agents can run simultaneously
- Isolated email processing per user

## 🆘 **Troubleshooting**

### **"Gmail Not Connected"**
- Ensure you entered correct Gmail address
- Must be @gmail.com
- Check internet connection

### **"No Drafts Generated"**
- Check you have unread emails
- Wait 5-10 minutes for agent to process
- Verify Gmail is connected

### **"Login Failed"**
- Ensure username and password are correct
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Try incognito/private mode
