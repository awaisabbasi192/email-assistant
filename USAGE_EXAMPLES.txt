💡 TIPS FOR MAXIMUM EFFICIENCY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TIP 1: BATCH PROCESS DRAFTS
  Don't review one at a time
  Batch review:
    1. Open Drafts page
    2. Review all pending (usually 10-20)
    3. Approve, edit, delete in batches
    4. Faster than one-by-one!

TIP 2: USE REGENERATE WISELY
  Regenerate when:
    • Tone is off
    • Missing important points
    • Confidence score is low
    • Want different approach

  Don't regenerate when:
    • Just a small edit needed
    • Tone is already good

TIP 3: CREATE CATEGORY-SPECIFIC TEMPLATES
  Make 2-3 templates for each common category:
    • Support: "Initial", "Follow-up", "Resolution"
    • Work: "Acknowledgment", "Meeting Request", "Feedback"
    • Personal: "Catch-up", "Plans", "Thanks"

TIP 4: MONITOR ANALYTICS WEEKLY
  Check Analytics page every Monday:
    • Which categories had most emails?
    • Processing rate trend up or down?
    • Average response time improving?
    • Use data to improve workflows

TIP 5: TRUST BUT VERIFY
  AI is 85% accurate but not perfect:
    • Always review before sending
    • Check tone matches your style
    • Verify facts are correct
    • Add personal touch when needed

TIP 6: USE KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
  If you're reviewing many drafts:
    • Tab to navigate between buttons
    • Enter to confirm actions
    • Alt+E to Edit
    • Alt+A to Approve
    • Alt+D to Delete

TIP 7: COMBINE WITH GMAIL FILTERS
  Use Gmail filters + Email Assistant together:
    • Gmail auto-labels important emails
    • Email Assistant sees labels
    • Automatically prioritize high-value emails
    • Focus on what matters most

TIP 8: SET SCHEDULE
  Check Drafts page at set times:
    • Morning: 9 AM (overnight emails)
    • Noon: 12 PM (morning emails)
    • Evening: 5 PM (afternoon emails)

  Gets emails processed by end of day!
